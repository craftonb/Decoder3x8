basic library for controlling 3x8 decoder. make sure your the values you contstruct your 
class object with are wired correctly in your circuit. 
 
example:
Decoder3x8 object(7,6,5,4);
7 should be connected to your third bit
6 should be connected to your second bit
5 should be connected to your first bit
4 should be connected to your enable 

if still dont understand look at example.
